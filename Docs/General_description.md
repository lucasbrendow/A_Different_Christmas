
# General Description — A Different Christmas

This project is an internal administrative system to operate a Christmas donation campaign for underprivileged children. It centralizes the registration of children and their guardians, supports sponsor assignment, and provides clear tracking of gift fulfillment and delivery progress.

The platform is login-protected and initially restricted to authorized administrative users. It is designed to make daily campaign work easier by replacing fragmented spreadsheets and manual follow-ups with structured registration, flexible grouping, and actionable reporting.

Key capabilities include:

- Registration and management of children (primarily ages 0–12, with exceptions when necessary) and mandatory association to at least one guardian
- Quick guardian lookup during child registration, with the ability to create a new guardian inline without breaking the flow
- Sponsor registration (in a later stage) and association of sponsors to one or more children
- Campaign visibility through filtered lists and reporting by status, neighborhood, sponsor, and custom groups
- Tracking of key states such as sponsor assignment, gift pending/received, and delivery completion
- Generation of A5 printable child labels containing essential gift-related information, plus an administrative label editor to customize layout and variables
- A dashboard with indicators and charts for overall campaign progress and deadlines
- Sponsor communication support, including reminders and the ability to share child labels as images
